# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Camera names and locations
- SSH hosts and aliases
- Preferred voices for TTS
- Speaker/room names
- Device nicknames
- Anything environment-specific

## Examples

```markdown
### Tools Setup

- **ffmpeg:** `C:\Users\Nguyendd\.openclaw\workspace\ffmpeg.exe`
- **ffprobe:** `C:\Users\Nguyendd\.openclaw\workspace\ffprobe.exe`
- **ImageMagick:** `C:\Users\Nguyendd\.openclaw\workspace\imagemagick\magick.exe`

### Activated Skills (Free)
- **Weather:** Current conditions and forecasts.
- **Canvas:** Dynamic UI elements and data visualization.
- **Healthcheck:** System security and status audits.
- **Session Logs:** Searchable history of our interactions.
- **Video Frames:** Extracting imagery from video files (using ffmpeg).
- **PDF Tools:** Reading and processing PDF documents.
- **Skill Creator:** Building new capabilities for Uranus.
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes, and share skills without leaking your infrastructure.
